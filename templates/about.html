What is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based tax that is levied on every value addition. It has replaced many indirect taxes that previously existed in India. GST aims to simplify the taxation process, making it easier for businesses to comply with tax regulations.
