Help

Welcome to the help page of GST Calculator India. Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about using our tool.

How to Calculate GST?

To calculate GST, simply enter the amount, select the GST rate, and choose whether the amount is inclusive or exclusive of GST. Then click the 'Calculate' button to see the GST amount and the total amount including GST.

Understanding GST Types

Exclusive GST: The GST amount is added to the original cost.

Inclusive GST: The GST amount is included in the total cost.
