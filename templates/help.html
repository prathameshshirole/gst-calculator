Help

If you need assistance using our GST calculator tool, please refer to the following instructions:

How to Use the GST Calculator

  1. Enter the amount in the input field.
  2. Select the appropriate GST rate from the dropdown menu.
  3. Choose whether the GST is inclusive or exclusive of the amount entered.
  4. Click the "Calculate" button to see the GST amount and the total amount.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is GST?
A: GST stands for Goods and Services Tax, which is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based tax that is levied on every value addition.

Q: How do I know which GST rate to apply?
A: The GST rate depends on the type of goods or services. Common rates are 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. You can refer to the GST rate schedule for detailed information.

Q: Can I use this calculator for both inclusive and exclusive GST?
A: Yes, our calculator supports both inclusive and exclusive GST calculations. Simply choose the appropriate option when entering your amount.
